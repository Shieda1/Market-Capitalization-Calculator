What is the market capitalization calculator?
The market capitalization calculator is a tool that computes the total value of a company's outstanding shares.
How to calculate market capitalization?
To calculate market capitalization you should use the following formula:
market capitalization = price of a single share * number of outstanding shares
Why use market capitalization calculator?
The market capitalization calculator is used in order to determine the total value of a company's outstanding shares.
